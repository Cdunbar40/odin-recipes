Chicken Pinapple Stir Fry

Description

This Chicken Pineapple Stirfry recipe is quick and easy to make, and only requires a few ingredients. Ensure that you have a large wok handy, because in this house we cook in bulk. I don't really like waxing poetic about food, so let's get into it. Side note: I am not a good chef - You've been warned.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs (2 lbs)
  • Red Bell Pepper (1)
  • White Onion (1)
  • Canned Pinapple Tidbits (2 cans)
  • Minced Garlic (2 Tblsp)
  • Soy sauce (1 Cup)
  • Hoisin Sauce (1/3 Cup)
  • Sesame Oil (1 Tblsp)
  • Corn Starch (As desired)
  • Salt and Pepper (As desired)

Steps

  1. Put the sesame oil into the wok and let the wok heat over medium high heat.
  2. Cut your chicken thighs into bit sized pieces and brown them in the wok. Season them as they cook.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, dice your onion and bell peppers.
  4. When the chicken is nearly done, add the minced garlic and stir until fragrant. Feel free to add more than 2 table spoons.
  5. Add the bell peppers, onion, pineapple tidbits (with the juice), soy sauce, and hoisin sauce. Stir the ingredients together, put a lid on the wok, and let simmer for at least half an hour.
  6. Allow the liquid to cook down as desired. Add corn starch to thicken the sauce as needed
  7. Serve over rice
